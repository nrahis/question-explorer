Whether a kid is curious about space, math, animals, or just wants to chat, Question Explorer allows access to a variety of popular AI models, with custom prompts created specifically with children between the ages of 8-12 in mind.

To get started, get an API key from your provider of choice and save it in the Settings area (keys aren't stored on the server backend, but to your device's local storage). Have your child choose their interests in the Customize area for a more tailored experience.

How to Use

Ask: Type any question in the search box and click "Ask Away!" to get your answer.

Chat: Switch to chat mode to have a conversation instead of just asking individual questions.

Favorites: When you find an answer you like, click the star to save it to your favorites.
